Professional Profile

I work in the information technology and digital transformation field for a global restaurant enterprise, focusing on how technology can genuinely improve operational efficiency and organizational capability.

In recent years, I’ve led teams navigating the complexity of the China market, exploring the practical implementation of modern architecture, intelligent operations, and data‑driven decisions. More than system building itself, my goal is to make the technology team an integral part of business innovation.

Professional Philosophy

I believe that great systems are not managed into existence — they are understood into existence. The true value of technology lies not in its complexity, but in how it helps an organization operate with greater clarity and rhythm.

For me, digital transformation is not merely about building systems — it is a change in how an organization thinks and learns. It’s about understanding, collaboration, and growth — making change itself a core capability.
